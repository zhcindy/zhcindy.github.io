About Me

Nice to meet you! I'm currently studying at Northwestern University in Evanston, IL and I'm set to graduate in June 2017 with a Bachelor's degree in Mechanical Engineering and the Segal Design Certificate.

I'm passionate about sharing the things that give me awe, like purposeful design, cool technology and artistic expression. My interests are in mechanical design, 3D printing technologies, and product design.

In the past, I've worked as a design intern with Feinberg School of Medicine's Simulation Lab, and as a mechanical design intern with S&C Electric Co. Aside from mechanical engineering, you might find me teaching youth, dancing, making music, and sharing my newest finds with others.
