About Me

Nice to meet you! I'm a mechanical engineer and designer based in Chicago, IL. I currently work as an engineer at Focal Point, a premier lighting company, where I design custom light fixtures.

I'm passionate about sharing the things that give me awe, like purposeful design, cool technology, and artistic expression. My interests are in mechanical design, 3D printing technologies, and product design. Aside from engineering, you might find me mentoring youth, dancing, making music, and sharing my newest finds with others.

Some of my past experiences include working on consumer products for Newell Brands and medical simulations for Feinberg School of Medicine's Innovation Lab. I received my Bachelor's in Mechanical Engineering and the Segal Design Certificate from Northwestern University in Evanston, IL.
