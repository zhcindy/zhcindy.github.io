About Me

Nice to meet you! I'm a mechanical engineer and designer based in Chicago, IL. I am currently a Project Mechanical Engineer at a premier lighting company.

I'm passionate about sharing the things that give me awe, like purposeful design, cool technology and artistic expression. My interests are in mechanical design, 3D printing technologies, and product design.

I received my Bachelor's degree in Mechanical Engineering and the Segal Design Certificate from Northwestern University in Evanston, IL. In the past, I've worked at as a design engineer on consumer products, as a design intern with Feinberg School of Medicine's Simulation Lab, and as a mechanical design intern with S&C Electric Co. Aside from mechanical engineering, you might find me teaching & mentoring youth, dancing, making music, and sharing my newest finds with others.
