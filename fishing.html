A 4-person, 23-week design capstone project that created the initial concept of a beginner's fishing kit for retail sale.

    My Role

  • Designed and prototyped multiple iterations of a new beginner-friendly two-piece bite indicator component
  • Engaged with users, retail stores, and industry professionals for design research and reviews
  • Created CAD models and visual aids to support communication of ideas to clients, classmates, and reviewers.

The Starting Point: Defining the Problem

This project started with our client, who was a professional fisherman with a new business idea to create a beginner's fishing kit to be sold in retail stores. His motivation came from observing that many users who attempted to fish for the first time were disappointed with their experience, and believes that a beginner-friendly product is missing in the current day market. Through interviewing shop owners, customers, peers and beginner students at a local fishing school, we identified the problem regarding failed beginner fishing experiences to be twofold:

  1. a lack of access to knowledge needed to confidently fish for the first time
  2. a lack of beginner-friendly equipment that is well designed to successfully catch fish

Our Solution

Our solution is a beginner's fishing kit, RIGGed, that bridges the gap between our users' needs and the current market offerings. It is an all-in-one kit that includes the essentials for a user to begin fishing for the first time. The fishing tackle inside the kit are pre-rigged and easy to put together with the fishing rod and reel.


RIGGed addresses our objectives through its 3 main components:

  • an intuitive tackle box embeds helpful information and visual cues for how to use the equipment
  • a novel two-piece bite indicator allows for pre-rigged leaders and accomodates for safety concerns
  • other essential curated components are included to put expert-picked and effective fishing gear in a beginner's hands

Bite Indicator

My focus was on the design and development of the two-piece bite indicator. It's role was to separate the pre-rigged leaders from the rod and reel when stored away. It also allowed for easy assembly for the user when using the kit. We designed the bite indicator using feedback from user testing, performance testing, and client input. It became a 5-piece design so that the design is injection moldable. We created CAD models and 3D printed multiple iterations for further testing.
