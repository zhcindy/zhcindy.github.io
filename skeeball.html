Skeeball Mechanics

An individual final project for a dynamics course that focused on mathematically modeling the motion of a ball rolled by an arm.

Mechanics Setup

The mechanic equations of motion were derived and visualized in an animation using Mathematica. The following factors were included in the derivations:

  • triple pendulum model for the throwing arm
  • Euler-Lagrange equations of motion with external forces for air drag
  • conserved Hamiltonian and additional external forces for impacts with the ground

For more information, please refer to the final report.
